1. Entity : Country, Province, City, Address, District
2. Attribute :	- Country(country_id, name)
		- Province(province_id, name, country_id)
		- City(city_id, name, province_id)
		- District(district_id, name, city_id)
		- Address(address_id, province_id, city_id)
	
3. Relasi : - Country(one to many ke Province & Address)
			- Province(one to many ke Address & City)
			- City(one to many ke Address & District)
			- District (one to many ke Address)
